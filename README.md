# Digital-Design-and-Branding-Portfolio
A comprehensive portfolio of digital design assets, featuring advanced photo manipulation, dynamic lighting composites, and commercial branding.  A collection of creative media projects showcasing precision vector pathing, advanced Photoshop retouching, and commercial layout design.

**Author:** Muhammad Shayan Shahbaz  
**Primary Tools:** Adobe Photoshop, Canva  

---

## Core Design Competencies

* **Advanced Photo Manipulation:** Seamlessly blending multiple assets, color grading, and executing complex lighting setups to create high-fidelity composites.
* **Precision Pathing & Masking:** Utilizing the Pen Tool for flawless subject isolation, intricate vector paths, and non-destructive layer masking.
* **Commercial Branding:** Designing professional sponsorship proposals, digital marketing assets, and cohesive branding kits for commercial clients.
* **Layout & Typography:** Balancing visual hierarchy, spatial alignment, and typography for highly readable, modern digital interfaces and print media.
